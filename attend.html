Attend Zion Talkz

We, Zion Talkz always centralized our ideas on ways to be a catalyst to the students in choosing their careers and pursuing their dreams. Zion Talkz is a great way to improve knowledge and construct their minds to up-grow their career.


We chose online platforms as a mode of communication in this pandemic situation, which can be accessed by everyone irrespective of the region they live in.


The onset of technology paved the way to work virtually, off-shoring or telecommuting and along with it is virtual meetings and conferences. This technology came into the limelight due to the pandemic situation which we all are facing now. Virtual sessions offer a wide range of advantages to the audience.


  • Easy access: Being it online, people from anywhere in the world can attend the session.
  • Time-saving: Setting up an online meeting saves plenty of time. It can reduce the time of traveling to another place.
  • Cost-effectiveness: It doesn't require the attendees to pay for traveling and accommodation costs.
  • Communication ability: A person attending the meeting will have the chance to improve their productive relationship.
  • Flexibility: The session can be recorded and provided to the attendees; this makes them re-listen the points.

The audience can approach us through our social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and so on. We have the total information about the ongoing sessions and future sessions on our official social media pages and the official Zion Talkz website.

Students and the audience can register for our sessions with the registration link provided on the official Zion Talkz sites. Our team will contact you through the mail and share the session link. You can access the session through the link at the time specified for the talk. Every participant will be given chance to interact with the speaker and gain more insights and clear their doubts.

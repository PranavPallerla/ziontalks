Help / FAQs

The Most Frequently Asked Questions are provided below. If you have any queries regarding Zion Talkz Sessions, feel free to contact us through any Social Media sites.



How to register for upcoming sessions in Zion Talkz?
You can register for upcoming sessions through our official Zion Talkz social media pages or our official webpage. After registering you will be getting a confirmation mail and a link for the session will also be mailed. A reminder will be sent again an hour before the session to ensure no one misses them.
Can we register for two or more sessions at the same time?
Yes, a student can register to any number of sessions at one go. All the upcoming sessions and its details will be available on the official sites and students can register using the link provided by filling out their details.
What is the procedure for selecting speakers for sessions?
We have a unique way of choosing the speakers for the talks. The selection of speakers entirely depends on the students by their votes. This process is done through social media sites where they can reach us and share their interests with us. Votes will be segregated and the decision finalized will be officially announced before a week. This process of opting for the speaker will allow the students to decide what they want to listen about. Between the sessions, we will have two-minute speeches help to warm up students and let them expand their thinking abilities.
Can students choose topics for Zion Talkz sessions?
Yes, of course. Students can suggest what kind of sessions they would wish to listen to. Based on the suggestions and recommendations we get, Our Zion Talkz team will definitely make necessary arrangements to have that particular session as soon as possible with great efficiency.
Do we get a participation certificate?
Every student who participates in the session gets a digital participation certificate. Make sure you have entered the correct credentials while filling out the registration form.
Where can I watch the previous Zion Talkz sessions?
All the previous session videos will be available on our Zion Talkz YouTube channel. We are making necessary arrangements to make it possible for students to access our previous session on the BookMyShow website too.
Will, there be any offline Zion Talkz sessions?
Zion Talkz is all conducted online for time being due to the pandemic situation. We are making use of many virtual speaking platforms to conduct Zion Talkz sessions. But we are planning to have offline sessions i.e. Face-to-Face interaction sessions in the future when everything falls back to place with utmost care and precautions.

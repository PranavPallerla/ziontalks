" Speaking passionately from the very centre of who you are is compelling, forceful, persuasive: That’s what leadership sounds like."

Zion Talkz is a community, welcoming people from every discipline and field to share their knowledge on the discipline they are specialized in—to every student who seeks a deeper understanding of the path they have chosen. We believe passionately in the power of a mentor to guide students down the path they have chosen for their future, to change attitudes, lives, and, ultimately, the world. We centralize our ideas in the fields of Business, Science, and Technology where the speakers share their experiences and innovations

Motto

  • Here we are going to present a platform where industry experts from various fields come up with new and intriguing technical topics to talk about.
  • We focus on inducing interest in students through our informative and exciting topics for all the branches.
  • It’s a virtual program to do something great for students in this pandemic situation.
  • We bring mentors and speakers—to the students—who are willing to join hands with us to help us realize this idea into reality.

Knowledge shared is knowledge squared. It is a power that can transform all spheres of your life and activity. It can be acquired in many different ways and from many sources, but not limited to perception or a reason. Zion Talkz is a great way to improve knowledge and construct their minds to up-grow their career. It helps them to boost their creativity and broaden perspectives towards living. These insightful talks can help you pick yourself up after a setback and grow toward success. We have a unique way of dealing with the audience by choosing the topic that triggers in everyone's minds. As our employees are from different backgrounds, we focus on interdisciplinary acts in which we can grab a huge audience. The speakers which we invite are often a pioneer of a certain skill or a budding entrepreneur as the challenges they faced are immense and there is a lot of learning in them to acquire from them. We do provide the attendees with the recorded version of our meeting to re-listen the points which they have missed during the meet. These sessions will be very helpful for people to grasp a new skill and gain knowledge over interesting topics. Lastly, we have an interaction period in which the audience is encouraged to clarify their queries and can also further contact them with our sources if they wish to associate for future needs.
